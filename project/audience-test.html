Audience Test

Tasks

  1. Navigate around the title page and find the "About" and "More Information" pages.
  2. Start the game and proceed to play until you hit options to pick from. Pick an option and continue the game.
  3. Play the game in fullscreen mode and exit out of the mode.

Questions

  • How easy is it to navigate through the site and find out information or play the game? (Were the buttons and links too small or hard to find?)
  • Does the content of the game depict a realistic story of how someone suffering from depression might go through?
  • Did you learn anything new you didn't know before?
  • Do you think there is anything missing from this site?
